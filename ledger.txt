PRISMIC HANDSHAKE LEDGER
PROTO_VERSION: 1

Definitions:
- Entry hash = sha256 of the entry block (ENTRY_ID through BODY, excluding HASH line, with trailing newline).
- Chain head hash = HASH value of the latest entry.
- Ledger is append-only. No entry may be modified once committed.

Format:
ENTRY_ID: ####
UTC: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ
PREV: (hash of previous entry, or GENESIS)
TYPE: handshake / checkpoint / note / discrepancy
SIGNER: (optional human/model identifier)
BODY: (single line, no internal newlines)
HASH: (sha256 of THIS entry block excluding the HASH line)
